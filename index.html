About Me

My name is Harrison Engoren and I am from a small town in West Surbuban Chicago called River Forest. Inspired by new experiences, I chose to study at the University of South Carolina and decided to major in Computer Science. Before long, I discovered that many aspects of computing are much more similar to everyday events than I had previously imagined. For example, challenges to design an elegant solution is often like being faced with a friend's puzzling riddle. Consider this:

There is a problem you've never heard of, but you are aware that there are certain constraints. Although others may not know the answer either, it is better (and more fun) to work together towards a solution. You will be frustrated for hours after what appearead to be a simple problem turned into a consuming conunudrum until finally, you rejoice when you discover the answer. The exasperation you felt as you mindlessly searched for an answer not only transformed into pride and confidence, but generated a thirst for the next puzzle.
